Why Boom Beach is better than Clash of Clans...



Date: 28th of September 2023.
First, I'm not saying that Clash is bad or else. I do like Clash of Clans, infact I still play the game after years I have downloaded it (I'm a proud TH12 village by the way üòÅ).

That been said... I have been playing Boom Beach for almost as much time, and I can say with confidence that Boom Beach is better than Clash of Clans.

Let me explain...

More buildings

Both the games have really good looking villages, but Boom Beach has a wider variety of buildings.

Other than cannons, towers and mortars, there are rocket launchers, shock launchers, machine guns, shield generators, laser beams, grapplers...
Boom Beach villages have no walls, which is actually a good thing, because there is far more room for placing buildings.

More... villages

On Boom Beach you can conquer resource bases to increment the production of wood, iron or stone needed for upgrading your buildings.
You cannot directly upgrade resource bases buildings because they will be upgraded after every new re-conquering.

More events

On Boom Beach there are different events happening during the week.

Monday Hammerman Strikes Back
Tuesday Dr. T Tropico Island
Wednesday Dr. T Volcano Island
Thursday War Factory
Friday Imitation Game
Saturday Dr. T Tropico Island
Sunday Dr. T Volcano Island


Other there are also monthly based events, like Warships and Mega Crabs.

Statues

Probably the coolest aspect of Boom Beach is the logic of Statues.

Statues are objects that give certain perks or bonuses to your village. They are classified in four colours.
Green Production bonuses: increases gold, wood, stone, iron production.
Red Troop bonuses: increases health or damage of troops.
Blue Defense bonuses: increases health or damage of buildings.
Purple Special bonuses: increases gun-bot energy, victory reward, or chances to get power stones.


Troops

On clash of clan every troop that has been deployed to the enemy village will no longer be available (except for heroes), even if it survives.
This is not the case for Boom Beach.

Boom Beach troops can be reused multiple times as long as they survive in the battlefield.
This allows to play multiple attacks consecutively, without having to wait for all the troops to be ready, if all of them survived.

To sum up

As I said in the beginning, there is nothing wrong with Clash of Clans.
It is an amazing game that I am still enjoying to play after so many years.
But I believe that Boom Beach has a more diversified and rich gameplay, with some really cool mechanics that, at the end of the day, I think they make the game far more interesting.
